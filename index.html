Infidel Express

pure rock-n-roll

The story of INFIDEL EXPRESS started in 2015 when future members of the band first met in Moscow and decided to form an international project with no limits in regards of cultures and political backgrounds. The first single "Can You Really Hear The Song" was issued on the day Turkey hit Russian military plane which led to a long cold conflict between the governments of the countries.

The idea of making peace by following the ­punk/­hardcore approach continued to evolve and led to a 8-­track debut album "The Riot Starts When You Are Young" which was recorded live and first performed in MIAM Studio Istanbul during the local riot uprising events and gas attack from the government forces.

Soon after the album was mixed and mastered in Moscow but in particular days when it was scheduled to be issued government coup attempt happened in Turkey so it had to be postponed in respect for the victims of the conflict. Release finally happened in 2016.

Despite problems with Visa and traveling between the countries Infidel Express continued to preserve the style of playing jambased songs in various punk locations like abandoned kindergartens and warehouses in Russia, Belarus, Ukraine, Turkey and local venues as well.

After 2017 Russian journey video split "DEAL" was recorded in Moscow with three new songs influenced by more dark grind/crust sound and the band evolved to even more intense and honest stage show presence with a lot of various atmospheric visual effects.

Infidel Express shared stage with a lot of awesome local bands such as: LifeLock, Tsygun, Minaret, Vorvan, KIWI and many others In 2018 trio started to be quartet as bass-guitar noise machine Slava Galkin (POHU1, DislLifeCunt) joined the band. Together new full-length album “Ultimate Plan” was composed in Istanbul and recorded in Moscow and going to be released in April 2020.
